A uniterrupted network means its drivers are running uninterruptedly. A connection to the device is based on the working of drivers involved. For a network connection during system breakdown its drivers might get damaged. To provide you these drivers at ease Network Pro presents you with drivers required in case of system breakdowns. Basically two main drivers are involved with system in case of network connection, Rundll32.dll and sechost.dll. Here these drivers play a major role in connection setting.
